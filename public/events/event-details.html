About This Puja

Navgrah Puja is performed to reduce planetary doshas and bring peace, prosperity, health, and success. This puja balances the nine planets and removes obstacles from life.

Benefits

  • Removes negative planetary effects
  • Improves career and finances
  • Brings mental peace and harmony
