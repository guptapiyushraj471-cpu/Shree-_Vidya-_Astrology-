About Rudrabhishek Puja

Rudrabhishek is performed by bathing the Shiva Lingam with milk, honey, ghee, curd, sugar, water, and holy substances while chanting the powerful Rudra mantras from the Vedas.

Benefits of Rudrabhishek Puja

  • Removes negative energies and doshas
  • Brings mental peace and emotional stability
  • Improves health and longevity
  • Enhances prosperity and success
  • Grants divine blessings of Lord Shiva

Puja Procedure

  • Ganesh Puja and Sankalp
  • Abhishek with Panchamrit and holy items
  • Chanting of Rudra Mantras
  • Aarti and Prasad Distribution
