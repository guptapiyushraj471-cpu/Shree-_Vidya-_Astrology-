About Dhanvantari Puja

Lord Dhanvantari emerged during the Samudra Manthan holding the pot of Amrit. Worshipping him helps in healing diseases, strengthening immunity, and achieving physical and mental well-being.

Benefits of Dhanvantari Puja

  • Improves physical and mental health
  • Supports recovery from long-term illnesses
  • Increases immunity and vitality
  • Reduces medical and health-related stress
  • Blessings for longevity and wellness

Puja Procedure

  • Sankalp and Ganesh Puja
  • Dhanvantari Mantra chanting
  • Offering herbs, flowers, and prasad
  • Aarti and blessings
