Citrine (Sunela)

Citrine is known as the stone of wealth and success. It attracts prosperity, positivity, and abundance while boosting confidence, motivation, leadership qualities, and business growth. Citrine is also used as a substitute for Yellow Sapphire (Pukhraj).
